consciousness digitization

July 4, 2044

Progress was slow in Dr. Fausten's division, so I personally went there to work with them. Eleanor's sharp as always, though she seemed disengaged. I wonder if it's because of her mother's hospital bills.

We finally made it. The emulation system that can correctly mimic consciousness. Create and retain memories. Remember corporeal feelings. It only took every single bit of knowledge and skill we've got to figure out the finer algorithms.
